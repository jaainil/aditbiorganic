## Supply Chain Storage That Protects Your Product

A well-manufactured product can lose value quickly if it is stored improperly. Temperature extremes, humidity ingress, improper stacking, and disorganised inventory management are the most common causes of quality degradation between production and delivery.

Our warehouse and go-down facilities are designed to protect product integrity across the entire storage period.

## Facility Overview

### All-Weather Storage
Our warehouse is a fully covered, all-weather structure. Granular fertilizers — particularly those with organic components or high coating loads — are sensitive to moisture. Covered storage prevents rain ingress and significantly reduces ambient humidity effects on the product.

### Separate Zones for Raw Materials and Finished Goods
Raw materials and finished goods are stored in separate, clearly demarcated zones. This prevents cross-contamination and makes inventory management simpler for both our team and visiting clients or logistics partners.

### Dedicated Truck Bays
Loading and unloading operations happen at dedicated truck bays, keeping logistics movement organised and reducing congestion in the storage area. Multiple simultaneous loading operations can be handled without interference.

### Organised Stock Management
Stock is managed with batch identification, FIFO (First In, First Out) discipline, and clearly labelled storage positions. At any point, our team can identify which batch of which product is located where — and when it was received or produced.

## Storage for Client Products

We offer **bonded storage** for products manufactured at our facility that clients are not yet ready to dispatch. Product remains in our warehouse under your ownership until you issue a dispatch instruction.

This allows clients to:

- Manufacture in larger, more economical batches and draw down inventory as orders are placed
- Use our facility as a staging point for regional distribution
- Buffer against transport or demand volatility without holding inventory at their own premises

## Inventory Reporting

For clients with product stored at our facility, we provide periodic inventory reports showing:

- Current stock by batch and SKU
- Age of stock in warehouse
- Space utilisation
- Upcoming dispatch schedule

## Access for Partner Logistics

If you use your own transport and logistics partners for collection, we facilitate smooth access:

- Advance scheduling for loading slots
- Truck bay allocation to minimise wait times
- Documentation (dispatch note, batch CoA, weight certificate) prepared before vehicle arrival
