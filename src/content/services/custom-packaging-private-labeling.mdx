## Market-Ready, Branded, and Consistent

Production does not end when the granule is manufactured. It ends when the product is in the right bag, correctly labelled, accurately weighed, and securely sealed — ready for your distribution channel.

Our packaging service completes the cycle from granule to shelf-ready product under your brand.

## Packaging Formats We Support

### PP Woven Bags
The standard format for bulk fertilizer supply in India. Available in 25 kg, 40 kg, and 50 kg configurations. We can seal with your printed label or work with your printed bag supplier to fill bags you provide.

### HDPE Bags and Containers
Higher barrier packaging for moisture-sensitive products or premium market positioning. HDPE is preferred for export markets and for products where longer shelf life is required.

### Custom Formats
If your market or product requires a non-standard format — retail-sized sachets, small-holder packs, or specific packaging specifications for export compliance — we can accommodate custom requirements with advance notice.

## Private Labeling

We produce under your brand, completely. Your label, your product name, your company contact details. We do not add Adit Biorganic branding to privately labelled products.

The label design and regulatory compliance (FCO registration details, CIB&RC references where applicable, guaranteed analysis) are your responsibility. We will flag obvious compliance issues if we identify them, but label approval is with the client.

## Our Packaging Process

### Weighing
Semi-automated weighing ensures each bag is filled to within ±0.5% of target weight. Weights are recorded for batch documentation.

### Filling
Material is fed into bags through controlled filling equipment that minimises dust generation and product degradation. Granule integrity is maintained through the fill cycle.

### Sealing
Bags are heat-sealed or stitched based on the bag type. Seal quality is visually inspected before the bag moves to the stacking and palletising area.

### Labeling
Labels are applied (or pre-printed bags are used) per your specification. Batch number and production date are applied at this stage for traceability.

### Palletising and Wrapping
Finished bags are stacked on pallets to your specification and wrapped for transport stability. Pallet documentation is prepared for dispatch.

## Quality at the Packaging Stage

Common packaging failures — underfill, broken seals, label misalignment — create downstream problems that are difficult to trace. Our packaging operation is a monitored process, not a terminal step. Weight check data, seal inspection records, and label verification are part of our batch documentation.
