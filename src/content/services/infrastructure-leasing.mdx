## Manufacturing Capacity Without Capital Expenditure

Building or acquiring granular fertilizer manufacturing infrastructure is a significant capital decision. Land, granulation equipment, coating lines, drying systems, lab setup — the total investment required to reach even 50 MT/day capacity runs into crores.

Infrastructure leasing is a different model: you lease access to our production lines, run your own production team, and manufacture your proprietary formulas in our facility. The infrastructure investment is ours. The production control is yours.

## What Infrastructure Leasing Includes

### Production Line Access
Access to our granulation, coating, drying, and screening equipment on an agreed schedule. We can structure leasing as dedicated time blocks (e.g., specific days or shifts per week) or as capacity reservation against a minimum throughput commitment.

### Facility Utilities
Power, water, compressed air, and the physical plant infrastructure are included. You do not need to provision utilities separately.

### Warehouse Space
Raw material storage and finished goods storage space within our facility is allocated to your operation during the lease period.

### Health, Safety, and Compliance Infrastructure
Our facility operates under established safety protocols and environmental compliance. Your team operates within this framework, with support from our HSE personnel for facility-level compliance requirements.

### Lab Access (Optional)
For clients who require on-site quality testing during their production runs, access to our ISO 9001:2015 certified lab can be structured into the leasing arrangement.

## What You Bring

- Your production team (operators, supervisors, QC personnel)
- Your raw materials and active ingredients
- Your formulation parameters and batch records
- Your quality specifications and CoA requirements

## Who This Service Is For

**Brands with proprietary formulas they cannot share:** Some clients have formulations that are commercially sensitive and cannot be produced under a standard contract manufacturing arrangement where our team handles production. Infrastructure leasing allows you to maintain complete control over your formula while using our physical plant.

**Established brands needing capacity expansion:** If your existing facility is at capacity and you need additional production volume without building a second plant, leasing time on our lines gives you immediate throughput.

**International brands entering the Indian market:** Companies establishing Indian manufacturing to meet local content requirements or reduce import costs can use our facility as their Indian production base while they assess whether to build their own plant.

**R&D and new product development:** Access to production-scale equipment for new formula development and scale-up trials, without the overhead of running a full contract manufacturing arrangement.

## Commercial Structure

Infrastructure leasing is structured as a fixed monthly fee plus a variable component based on actual production throughput. Minimum commitment periods apply to allow us to plan facility scheduling effectively.

All commercial terms are confirmed in a formal Infrastructure Leasing Agreement before operations commence.

## Confidentiality

Under an infrastructure leasing arrangement, your formulation, production parameters, and output data remain entirely confidential to your team. Our facility staff are not involved in your production operations during your leased time.
