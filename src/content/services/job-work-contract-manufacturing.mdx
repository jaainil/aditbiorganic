## Full-Cycle Manufacturing Under Your Brand

Job work and contract manufacturing is the core of what Adit Biorganic offers to fertilizer brands across India. We function as your manufacturing arm — handling every step of production while you focus on sales, marketing, and customer relationships.

You bring the brand. We handle the factory.

## What "Full Cycle" Means

Our contract manufacturing service covers the entire production journey:

### Raw Material Sourcing
We source and inspect base materials (Gypsum, Dolomite, organic matter, micronutrients) against your specification. Our vendor relationships and purchasing scale often allow us to source at better prices than brands can achieve independently.

### Granulation
Base granules are produced to your specified size, density, and composition using our pan granulator and rotary drum systems. Every batch is made to a documented master batch record.

### Coating
Your active ingredients — liquids or powders — are applied onto the base granules with uniform coverage. Our coating infrastructure handles both bio-inputs and conventional chemistries.

### Drying and Screening
Granules are dried to target moisture levels and screened to remove oversize and undersize fractions. This ensures the material that reaches packaging meets your size distribution specification.

### Packaging
We pack finished product in your branded bags — PP woven, HDPE, or custom formats — with your label, weight, and regulatory information. Semi-automated weighing and sealing ensures consistency.

### Dispatch
Finished goods are stored in our warehouse with FIFO discipline and dispatched per your schedule. We provide batch documentation with every shipment.

## Who This Service Is For

**Fertilizer brands without a factory:** If you have a market, distribution network, and product formula but not manufacturing infrastructure, we are your manufacturing partner.

**Brands expanding into new product lines:** Testing a new granular product does not require building a new production line. We can produce trial batches and scale up once demand is confirmed.

**Seasonal overflow:** If your own facility cannot handle seasonal demand peaks, we provide additional contracted capacity without the capital cost of expansion.

**Import-substitution brands:** Companies building Indian alternatives to imported specialty fertilizers can use our facility to develop and produce domestic formulations.

## Confidentiality and IP Protection

We maintain strict confidentiality on all client formulations. Your recipe, your active ingredients, and your product specifications are not shared — internally or externally — beyond the production team handling your order.

## Minimum Order and Lead Times

We work with clients to establish minimum batch sizes that are commercially viable on both sides. For new clients, we offer trial batches before committing to regular supply agreements.

Lead times are confirmed at the point of order and depend on raw material availability and production scheduling. Most standard formulations are producible within agreed windows; novel formulations require validation time.
