## Beyond Standard Bentonite Fillers

Most granule manufacturers use bentonite as their base — an inert filler that holds the shape of the granule but contributes nothing to the crop or the soil. At Adit Biorganic, we take a different approach.

We manufacture what we call **Recipe Granules**: bases that are themselves formulated from functional materials — Gypsum, Dolomite, organic compost, or combinations thereof — specifically chosen to match your product's performance requirements.

## What We Manufacture

### Mineral Base Granules

Bases formulated with Gypsum and Dolomite provide secondary nutrients (Calcium, Magnesium, Sulphur) as an inherent part of the carrier. Your active ingredient gets coated onto a granule that is already adding value to the soil.

### Organic Base Granules

Bases formulated from composted organic matter provide organic carbon, support soil microbial activity, and comply with organic certification requirements. Ideal for bio-stimulant, bio-fertilizer, and bio-pesticide coatings.

### Custom Recipe Granules

We work with your R&D team to develop a base granule specification matched to your formulation goals — pH target, density, porosity, coating absorption capacity, or specific nutrient profile.

## Our Coating Capabilities

Our granules are engineered to hold **over 70% coating load** — significantly higher than typical bentonite-based granules. This allows:

- Higher active ingredient concentration per granule
- Better uniformity of coating distribution
- Lower coating breakdown during handling and transport

We can coat your product with:

- Liquid bio-pesticides and bio-stimulants
- Powder actives (micronutrients, fungicides, insecticides)
- Multi-layer coatings for controlled release

## Quality Assurance

Every production batch goes through our ISO 9001:2015 certified in-house lab:

- Raw material specification check before production begins
- In-Process Quality Control (IPQC) during granulation and coating
- Finished goods analysis (size distribution, moisture, bulk density, coating load)
- Batch Certificate of Analysis issued with every dispatch

## Scale and Capacity

Our facility operates 24/7 with a production capacity of **190+ MT/day**. We support both regular scheduled supply for established brands and trial batch production for new product development.
